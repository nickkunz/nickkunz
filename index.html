About Me

Nick Kunz is a Graduate Student at Columbia University. His research interests are at the intersection of analytics, statistics, and machine learning methods in geography, real estate, finance, and economics.

Most recently, he was a Development Analyst at Brawner & Company in Snoqualmie, WA, a boutique real estate development consulting firm specializing in multifamily affordable housing development finance. Prior, he served as a full-time Volunteer at AmeriCorps and Design Fellow at bcWORKSHOP in Dallas, TX from 2013 to 2014, and was a Design Consultant at n Creative Studios in Phoenix, AZ from 2011 to 2013.

He earned a Bachelor of Science in Design (Hons), summa cum laude, in 2012 from Barrett, The Honors College at Arizona State University.
